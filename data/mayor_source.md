# Mayor N — synthetic source corpus

> Synthetic demo persona for The Honest Mirror. Mayor of a town of ~60,000, second year
> in office. Constructed so that the **stated self** (interviews, public framing) diverges
> from the **enacted self** (private notes under pressure) on one clear value axis
> (control ↔ trust) plus a lighter second axis (openness). Reviewer can run this corpus
> end to end. Not a real person — no consent issue, no proprietary material.
>
> Atomised (selectively) into `mayor_atoms.metta`. Provenance tags in [brackets] map
> source fragments to atoms.

## Interview 1 — Intake (month 1) · STATED self

*Facilitator: How would you describe the way you lead?*

[i1-team] "I genuinely see myself as a team person. The council isn't me — it's all of us. I'd never want to be the mayor who decides everything alone; I trust the people around me and I lean on them."

[i1-listen] "Listening to residents is the heart of it. Before any big decision I want the room's voice, not just mine. That's non-negotiable for me."

[i1-develop] "Part of the job is growing the next people. I try to hand things over, let my deputies own real decisions, even when it's uncomfortable."

[i1-open] "And I want to lead openly — no pretending I have it all figured out. If I'm struggling, the team should be able to see that. Vulnerability isn't weakness."
